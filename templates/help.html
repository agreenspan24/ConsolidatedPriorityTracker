Instructions on how to use Consolidated

  • Log-in with missouridems.org gmail account

  • Choose the office and pages

  • Click “Open”

 

Pages

  • Same Day Confirms

    • Enter Vols/interns’ first name, last name and cell phone

    • Select shift time and activities

    • Click “Add Walk In”

    • Change volunteers’ status according to the confirm calls

      • Conditions:

        • If vols/interns confirmed to do a later shift on the same day, change the status to “Same Day Confirmed”;

        • If vols/interns arrived at the field office, change the status to “In”;

        • If vols/interns completed their shifts, change the status to “Completed”;

        • If vols/interns did not show up at their shift times, change the status to “Flake”;

        • If vols/interns are not able to complete their shifts, change the status to “Declined”;

    • Other: Add notes and hit “Enter”

 

  • KPH

    • When vols/interns come to the field office, change theirs status on Same Day Confirm Page to “In”

    • Move to the KPH page

    • Enter volunteers’ names, goal, # Packets Given, and Packets Info

    • Click “Create Canvassing Group”

    • Click “Depart” when volunteers/interns leave the field office

    • Record the number of doors completed during check-ins

    • Change actual doors

    • Click “Returned” when volunteers/interns returned finished packets

    • Other: Add notes and hit “Enter”

 

  • Flake Chase

    • If vols/interns did not show up at scheduled times, change the status to “flaked” on Same Day Confirms page

    • Move to Flake Chase Page

    • Change status according to flake chase calls

    • Move back to “Same Day Confirms” page

    • Check the updated status

      • Conditions

        • If flake chase calls are completed but the volunteers did not reschedule shift times, status on the “Same Day Confirms” page will be updated to “Declined” correspondingly;

        • If flake chase calls are completed and the volunteers rescheduled their shifts, status on the “Same Day Confirms” page will be updated to “Scheduled”;

        • If flake chase calls are completed and the volunteers scheduled later shifts on the same day, the status on the “Same Day Confirm” page will be updated to “Scheduled”;

        • If the volunteers are not flake chased, the status on the “Same Day Confirm” page remains “Flaked”;

    • Other: add flake chase notes and hit “Enter”

  • Review

    • Check the summary of today’s activity results

    • Sync to VAN
